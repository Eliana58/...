SmOOthie Bar

Smoothies are actually drinks with a thick and creamy texture, which combine fruits or vegetables, fruit juices, yogurt, milk, water or ice and the addition of nuts, seeds, seeds and more. The simplest smoothie contains two ingredients: a solid base - fruit or vegetable, and a liquid - water, milk or yogurt. These are usually accompanied by additional raw materials. The variety of smoothies that can be prepared is large and colorful, and one of its advantages is the possibility to combine fruits and vegetables that are not in season, such as frozen berries. Fresh fruits and vegetables are not necessarily better quality and healthier than frozen ones. Most often even the opposite. The nutritional quality of the frozen ones will be higher, as they have undergone a quick infusion and freezing near the time of harvest, and the nutritional profile of the vegetable or fruit is largely maintained as it was at the time of harvesting.
